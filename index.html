Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Habibur Rahaman Fahim. I graduated from North South University with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering. My main interests are in artifical intelligence, machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and cloud computing. I enjoy solving complex problems, experimenting with new technologies, and building AI-driven solutions.

As a data scientist and AI engineer, I work with machine learning and deep learning to develop intelligent systems. I'm proficient in C++, Python and Java, along with other programming languages. I also have experience in AI-driven cloud computing and computer architecture, focusing on building scalable AI models and optimizing cloud-based infrastructures.

I also have experience in computer vision, applying deep learning to tasks like image classification, object detection, and image segmentation. These skills are valuable in industries like healthcare, autonomous systems, and security.

I'm an active member of the AI and developer communities, engaging on platforms like Stack Overflow, Reddit, Discord, and Medium. You can find my projects on GitHub and Kaggle, where I showcase my work and collaborate with others.
